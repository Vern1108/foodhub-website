Introduction

Bibingka De Macapuno

This is kind of similar with the traditional bibingka of Laguna, but it has Macapuno as a filling. You will love this Bibingka de Macapuno if you are macapuno lover. Their outlet is located at Brgy. San Juan, Santa Cruz, Laguna.

 

Bibingka De Macapuno Recipe

Ingredients

• 1 1/2 Macapuno

• Galapong

• Condensed Milk

• Sugar

• Butter

• Pandan

• Banana Leaf

 

 

Procedure:

1. In a sauce pan, add 1 ½ kg of crushed macapuno, water, pandan, and washed sugar then mix.

2. Cover and let it cook for 15 minutes.

3. After the mixture gets cooked, add galapong and condensed milk then mix them all together.

4. Get your banana leaf and brush a bit of butter on it.

5. Put your banana leaf on a baking pan then add the mixture.

6. Put your mixture in the oven and bake it for 1 ½ hour. Then serve.
